The regulatory perimeter is no longer a future negotiation — it is the present-day reality any frontier lab ships into.

AI 监管远比看上去复杂。这是 insane scale 的 prisoners dilemma。理论上如果全球所有 leading AI lab 同意同一套 review 和 slow-down 流程,frontier intelligence 会以相近的速率到达,capability 也会均匀扩散。如果 US 还在 frontier,它不会慢下来。

English

Levie follows up with the game-theory frame: 'AI regulation is far less simple than it looks. It's prisoners dilemma at insane scale.' AI regulation is far less simple than it looks. It's prisoners dilemma at insane scale.
