Theme 02

软件暗工厂：九成 token 将异步运行 / The software dark factory runs asynchronously

Factory CEO Matan Grinberg 预计，真正的下一阶段不是人类持续对话式调用 agent，而是 agent 根据客户信号自行开工；企业要接受这种自动化，同时保留模型独立与可迁移资产。

TD
Training Data Podcast
AI 播客
中文

Factory CEO Matan Grinberg 预计，未来十二到二十四个月，九成 token 会转成异步运行。agent 不再等开发者喊一句「去做这个」，而是自己从客户信号里发现问题、提出修复并交出第一版方案。

他把终点叫作软件的「暗工厂」：灯可以关着，系统仍在持续构建；人只把注意力留给少数高杠杆的设计与判断时刻。

未来十二到二十四个月，大约九成 token 会异步运行。

软件开发会走向一座暗工厂：即使关着灯，事情也会自行发生。

English

Factory CEO Matan Grinberg predicts that within twelve to twenty-four months, 90% of tokens will be asynchronous. Instead of waiting for a developer to say "hey, Codex" or "hey, Droid," agents will notice customer signals, propose fixes and produce first-pass solutions on their own.

He calls the destination a dark factory for software: the lights can be off while the system keeps building, with humans concentrating on the rare high-leverage design and judgment moments.

I think in twelve to twenty four months, like, 90% of tokens will be asynchronous tokens.

This idea of a dark factory where the lights are off and things are just happening, that is where software development's going.
