你可以挥挥手说，不，这就像电力。好吧。细想一下电力时代发生了什么？电气化之前的人也不傻。

English

Benedict Evans pushes back on the 'AI is like electricity' hand-wave. The deeper test: what happened with electricity? People before electrification weren't dumb either. The real question is which industries get restructured first, and which resist.

On the job-loss panic: Hinton's 10-year-old claim that we should stop training radiologists is the canonical example of smart people misunderstanding what those jobs actually involve. The pattern repeats with every new model release — pattern-match a headline job title, declare it dead, ignore the actual workflow.

You can wave your hands and say, no. This is like electricity. Okay. Fine. It's like electricity. Well, what happened with electricity? And there was a time before electricity, and people before electricity weren't dumb either.

We get deep into logical fallacies here. So we've got Jeff Hinton, was ten years ago, saying stop training radiologists. And the problem here here is he doesn't actually understand what radiologists do.
