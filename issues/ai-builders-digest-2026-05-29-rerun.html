Theme 03

Model Routing & the Cost Story / 模型路由与成本叙事

Token 预算开始进 OpEx,no-code 的底层假设被推翻——成本结构和工具分层正在被一起重写。

AL
Aaron Levie
Box CEO
@levie
中文

Aaron Levie 把一件正在发生的事点出来了:token 预算在 OpEx 里占比越大,model routing 就越会成为必然结论。这也是应用 AI 层最大的差异化战场之一。

原因是:一个领域最了解自己的工作模式,一个聪明的路由器可以让 80% 的调用走便宜模型,20% 真正需要前沿模型的请求才走贵的。这就是应用层的防御性护城河——不是模型的访问权,而是路由的智能。

跟开源模型的前沿放在一起读:能路由的模型越多,路由器本身越值钱。

Levie:token 预算进 OpEx 后,model routing 是必然结论,也是应用层最大差异化。

English

Aaron Levie names the inevitability: as token budgets take a larger share of operating expenses, model routing becomes the inevitable conclusion. This is also one of the biggest areas of differentiation for the applied AI layer.

The reason: a domain knows its own work patterns, and a smart router can pick the cheap model for 80% of calls and the frontier model for the 20% that actually need it. That is the applied layer's defensible moat — not access to the model.

Read this against the open-model frontier: a router is more valuable the more models you can route between.

As token budgets take on a larger part of operating expenses over time, model routing is the inevitable conclusion. This is also one of the biggest areas of differentiation for the applied AI layer over time. By understanding the different work patterns in your domain, and having a router pick the right model for the right call, you can drive down cost significantly.

The best founders treat the following as a necessary component of their business but NOT the entire business: > AI / timing / why now > Funding > Distribution / hype > Market > Product > Revenue. I'm seeing too many pitches that are touting only one of these as the core reason.
