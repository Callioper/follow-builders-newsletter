他对「美国开源 AI 落后于闭源」这个 frame 不买账。整条 AI 行业三个月就翻一页，开源闭源之间的具体 gap 没你想的那么要紧；他反而点名中国 open-source 社区，说正是他们的开放让后续所有公司都能做产品，这是过去几年被低估的贡献。

他给客户的 practical case 也很直白：每家公司都是围着「secret」建的——数据、workflow、客户上下文。AI 跟这个 secret 贴得越紧，价值越大；open weights 让你在保留 secret 的同时还能蹏 frontier 的能力，这就是为什么「为这个客户 fine-tune 一个模型」这事不是 demo 是 production。

如果你接受「我们已经在极限上跑」这件事是真的，那么获得更多智能的唯一路径就是做更高效的事。我们没法靠堆力拿到更多智能，前提条件是我们已经在极限上了。

我认为「某个国家的成果全是 copycat 出来的」这件事完全不成立，真的不是这样。

每家公司都是围绕一个 secret 建起来的——这个 secret 不只是 IP，还包括平台：它怎么处理问题、怎么跟客户互动、怎么想解决方案。AI 的价值永远跟它和这些 secret 的贴合度成正比，因为 AI 取决于数据。

English

Bryan Catanzaro (NVIDIA, head of Nemotron) on this week's MAD Podcast: the question he keeps returning to is whether we're already at the limit of compute — and if so, the only path to more intelligence is efficiency, not more force.

Catanzaro pushes back on the framing that US open-source AI is lagging behind closed models. He argues the gap matters less than the field's overall pace, and credits China's open-source community with making the open ecosystem viable for everyone downstream.

His pragmatic case for customers picking open weights: every company is built around a secret — its data, its workflows, its customer context. AI tied tightly to that secret is more valuable than AI locked behind an API. Open weights let you keep the secret while still benefiting from frontier capability.

If you accept as the truth that we're gonna be running at the limit, then what that means is that the way to get more intelligence is to be more efficient. We can't get more intelligence by applying more force if we're already at the limit.

I think it's absolutely false to say that, you know, the achievements of some other country are all being created by sort of, you know, copycat mentality. It's just not true.

Every company is built around a secret. This is a secret that has to do with not just their intellectual property, but also their platform... And it is always the case that the value of AI is greater when it can be more tightly connected with those secrets because AI depends on data critically.

聊到 Nemotron 的架构，Catanzaro 把 hybrid SSM + full attention 这条路讲得很具体：SSM 把整条 sequence 压成一个固定大小的 cache，擅长全局的、印象派的理解；full attention 是 lossless 的，擅长精确点出某个 token。两边各有强项，合起来比任一边单独用都更聪明——而且这不是速度 trick，是模型真的更聪明了。

Qwen 已经在用 hybrid SSM 路线，Kimi 推的叫 linear attention，几乎所有 frontier lab 都在往这个方向走。Nemotron 3 Ultra 这代能登顶 US open-weights 榜，hybrid 这套占了相当一部分原因。

讲 MoE 的时候 Catanzaro 给了一个很朴素的 frame：你想让模型记住整个互联网上的所有事，但你问它一个问题时，它真的需要把整个宇宙想一遍吗？其实不需要——所以 MoE 的本质就是这种 sparsity，大部分 activation 在任何一次 query 上都是浪费的。

它们的原理就是这样——不是任意看整条 sequence，而是每一步把全部内容压成一个 constant cache、一个小草稿区，然后基于这个工作。这种 constraint 反而让模型在一些任务上更聪明。

现在很多模型都在用 hybrid SSM 路线。Qwen 已经这么做了，Kimi 这段时间在用的是他们叫 linear attention 的方案。

MoE 是一种 sparsity。你想训练一个模型记住整个互联网、记住历史上所有事。但回答某个具体问题时，它真的需要把整个宇宙想一遍吗？其实不需要。

English

On Nemotron's architecture: Catanzaro explains why Nemotron uses a hybrid state-space model + full-attention stack rather than either alone. SSMs compress the whole sequence into a constant-sized cache — better at global, impressionistic understanding. Full attention sees everything losslessly — better at picking out exact tokens.

The combination is more than a speed trick. The model is actually smarter than either component alone, and most frontier labs are now adopting some flavor of hybrid (Qwen has it, Kimi has 'linear attention'). Catanzaro credits this work with part of Nemotron 3 Ultra landing as the #1 US open-weights model.

On MoE: 'you want it to remember absolutely everything about the history of everything. But when you're answering a particular question, does it seem reasonable that it needs to actually think about the entire universe?' The point of mixture-of-experts is exactly that sparsity — most activations are wasted on any single query.

That's how they work. Right? So instead of having the ability to look at the entire sequence randomly, they summarize everything at every step into a constant, cache or a little scratch pad that they're working on. And, that constraint seems to actually make them smarter at some tasks.

A lot of models these days are being built with hybrid SSM approaches. For example, QN has done that. Kimi is using what they call Kimi linear attention these days.

So mixture of experts is a form of sparsity. The idea is, wow, you wanna train a model on the entire Internet. You want it to remember absolutely everything about the history of everything. But when you're answering a particular question, does it seem reasonable that it needs to actually think about the entire universe in order to answer that question? Actually, no.

Levie 把接下来一段时间的 AI 结构讲成四层：frontier、open weights、applied、enterprise。applied 层的护城河是 harness——怎么把 frontier 当 manager、open weight 当 workhorse 的分工做对。他给了一个具体的实验：Fable 2x premium 团队跑下来，有效 delegation 反而把总成本降了。

Aaron Levie（Box CEO）把 AI 接下来一段时间的结构讲成了四层：frontier lab（数据 + compute + 突破）、open weights（快速吸纳 frontier 突破、可以「at cost」跑）、applied AI 层（frontier x open weights 组合，有自己的 RLed model），以及单个 enterprise 把自己的 context 接进任何一层。

他的 punchline 是这事根本不是零和——每一层都有空间。applied 层的工作就是把 frontier 智能和 open / 便宜 closed model 组合起来，拿住 eval set、domain context、enterprise 信任，并为高频可预测的任务跑自己的 RLed model。

讲一下我对接下来一段时间 AI 结构性走向的几个判断：frontier 智能会继续往前走；open weights 会快速吸纳 frontier 突破；applied AI 层在把 frontier 智能和 open 或便宜的 closed model 组合编排 workflow 这件事上有巨大机会；单个 enterprise 则主要聚焦于把自己的 enterprise context 接进去。

总结：即便其中一些事会被框成零和，但所有层和路径都有大量机会。

English

Aaron Levie (Box CEO) lays out the four-layer AI structure he expects to hold for the foreseeable future: frontier labs (data + compute + breakthroughs), open weights (rapidly absorbs frontier breakthroughs, lets you run 'at cost'), the applied AI layer (frontier x open weights, with their own RLed models), and individual enterprises wiring their context into any of the above.

His punchline is that this isn't zero-sum — every layer has room. The applied layer's job is to combine frontier intelligence with open or cheap closed models, hold the eval set + domain context + enterprise trust, and have its own RLed models for high-volume predictable tasks.

A few thoughts on what we will see in AI structurally for the foreseeable future: Frontier intelligence continues unabated... Open weights rapidly absorbs frontier breakthroughs... The Applied AI layer has a huge opportunity to combine frontier intelligence with open or cheap closed models to orchestrate workflows... Individual enterprises will generally focus on their enterprise context...

Net net: even though some of this gets framed as zero sum, there's just a ton of opportunity for all layers of the stack and approaches.
