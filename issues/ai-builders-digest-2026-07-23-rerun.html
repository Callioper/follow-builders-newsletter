Replit CEO
@amasad
中文

Amjad Masad 把模型路由的利益冲突说得很清楚：平台只要因导流某些模型而获利，「自动选择最优模型」就可能变成包装。路由层要成为可信基础设施，先得证明它替用户优化，而不是替供应商分发。

English

Amjad Masad argues that a model router is only credible if it is economically neutral. Once the operator is rewarded for steering traffic toward particular models, routing becomes a marketing facade rather than an optimization layer.

If you're incentivized to push certain models, your router is merely a facade.
