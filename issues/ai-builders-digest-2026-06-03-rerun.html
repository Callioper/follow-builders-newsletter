Harness Becomes the Moat / Harness 成为新护城河

真空回归的第一周,三家独立来源把同一句话再讲一遍:模型层会变成可替换件,harness + eval + context 才是真正决定谁能上 frontier 的那层。

A
Anthropic
Anthropic 官方博客
中文

Anthropic 发了一篇工程长文——[How we contain Claude across products],讲他们过去 12 个月怎么把内部 agent 部署的护栏一层层加厚。一年前会被直接驳回的那种访问级别,现在已经是常规操作,文章把让这件事变得安全的那些具体控制讲得很清楚。

核心心智模型是:blast radius × 失败概率。模型训练把概率压下去,safeguard 把爆炸半径罩住。文章里列的 containment 模式(限定权限、隔离环境、action 白名单、事后复核)读起来就像一份 harness 规格说明书。

把它当参考架构读,不是公关稿。

十二个月前,给 Claude 那种能搞垮 Anthropic 内部服务的访问权限,我们的标准答案是直接驳回。现在这种访问已经是日常,Anthropic 开发者因此效率更高。

English

Anthropic released a long engineering post — How we contain Claude across products — walking through twelve months of hardening their internal agent deployments. They now grant Claude access that would have been rejected out of hand a year ago, and they explain exactly which controls made that possible.

The mental model: blast radius multiplied by likelihood of failure. Model training drives likelihood down; safeguards cap blast radius. Their concrete list of containment patterns (scoped credentials, isolated environments, action allow-lists, post-hoc review) reads like a harness specification.

Read it as a reference architecture, not a marketing post.

Twelve months ago, we would have rejected out of hand the idea of granting Claude access sufficient to take down an internal Anthropic service. Today that level of access is routine, and Anthropic developers are more productive for it.
